Started as an idea, being manifested into a reality. Juniors at Rock Ridge (Neel C, Fady M) discussed the possibility of building the first ever FSAE car with the aerospace teacher (Mr.Johnson) at Rock Ridge. The 3 worked together and started building a plan on how they could accomplish this. They spent days and nights researching about the car and how they can perfect it. Works had already started a year before it was introduced to the public.

With the introduction to the public, people started to help out. Started out as a 3 people project turned to an effort by the entire school. With the additional support, the time that it would take to build the car decreased drastically.
